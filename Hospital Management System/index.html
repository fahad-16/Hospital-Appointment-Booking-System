Welcome to Our Hospital

This website serves as an online hospital appointment booking system where you can browse a list of doctors, review their specialities, and submit appointment requests through a simple form. The design focuses on showing information in a clear and organized way so that you can quickly understand their options and choose a suitable doctor and time. It is developed to demonstrate how a basic web‑based solution can make the appointment booking process more structured, efficient, and easy to use.

Main Features:

  • Patients can easily book a doctor’s appointment without standing in a long queue at the hospital.
  • Patients can see different doctors and their specialities, so they can choose a suitable doctor more confidently.
  • Patients can select a preferred date and time, which helps them plan their visit better and avoid unnecessary waiting.
  • It makes the overall experience more convenient for both patients and hospital staff, because basic information and appointment requests come through a clear, structured form.
